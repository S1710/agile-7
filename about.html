Who We Are

A Legacy of Financial Wisdom

Founded in 2001 in the financial heart of New Delhi, Agile Capital Services was born from a singular vision: to democratize institutional-grade wealth management for India's most ambitious individuals and enterprises.

Over two and a half decades, we have grown into one of India's most trusted independent financial advisory firms, managing over ₹12 billion in assets for clients across 30+ cities. Our philosophy is simple — your prosperity is our purpose.
