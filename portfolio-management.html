Overview

Professionally Managed
Portfolios for Superior Returns

Portfolio management is both an art and a science. At Agile Capital Services, our Portfolio Management division blends quantitative modelling, fundamental analysis, and real-time market intelligence to construct and actively manage portfolios that deliver consistent, risk-adjusted outperformance.

We offer both discretionary and advisory portfolio management services. In the discretionary model, our expert fund managers make all investment decisions on your behalf within predefined parameters, ensuring swift, disciplined execution without the need for your constant involvement.

Key Benefits
Active Portfolio Construction

Our research team identifies high-conviction opportunities across market caps and sectors, building concentrated portfolios with edge-driven stock selection that outperforms passive benchmarks.

Systematic Rebalancing

Portfolios are continuously monitored and rebalanced when allocations drift from targets — ensuring your risk exposure stays within defined parameters regardless of market movements.

Real-Time Performance Monitoring

Our client portal provides live portfolio analytics including P&L, XIRR, drawdown analysis, and sector exposure — giving you complete transparency at all times.

Benchmark-Beating Performance

Our model portfolios have consistently delivered superior returns compared to Nifty 50 and Sensex benchmarks over rolling 3, 5, and 10-year periods.
